SEO Title: EasyCampus FAQ | University ERP Questions

Answers for university leadership, IT teams, and operations heads

Clear responses to the questions institutions ask before replacing spreadsheets, legacy portals, and disconnected systems.

How long does implementation take?

Typical deployments range from 6 to 16 weeks based on modules, migration complexity, and stakeholder readiness.

Can EasyCampus support mentors and faculty separately?

Yes. The platform provides distinct role-based dashboards for admins, faculty, mentors, and students.

Does the platform support attendance thresholds and defaulter alerts?

Yes. Attendance rules, thresholds, and escalation policies are configurable by institution or department.

Can students use mobile access?

Yes. The product blueprint includes a React Native mobile app for attendance, marks, timetable, assignments, and notifications.
